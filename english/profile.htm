Academic Profile

Kensaku Gomi
engaged in researches in finite group theory (a branch of algebra) at the Department of Mathematics, the College of General Education, the University of Tokyo from Apr. 1973 to Mar. 1992, and
engaged in establishment of new mathematical psychology at the Graduate School of Mathematical Sciences, the University of Tokyo from Apr. 1992 to Mar. 2010 and at home from Apr. 2010 on,
and in the meantime
obtained a doctorate in mathematics at the University of Tokyo for the dissertation Characterizations of linear groups of low rank, J. Fac. Sci. Univ. Tokyo 23, 1976,
published the monograph MATHEMATICAL NOOLOGY: Intellectual Machines, Logic, Tongues and Algebra through WWW from Spring 2008 on,
published a related paper FOUNDATIONS OF EUSOPHY: A Mathematical Concept of God for the Reconciliation of Religion and Science through WWW from Christmas Day 2014 on,
published a related note FROM GRAPHS TO ALGEBRAS: From What We See to What We Penetrate through WWW from Feb.2026 on, and
published Eusophy — The supreme intellectual endeavor through WWW from Apr.2026 on.
