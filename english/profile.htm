academic profile

Kensaku Gomi
engaged in researches in finite group theory (a branch of algebra) at the Department of Mathematics, the College of General Education, the University of Tokyo from Apr. 1973 to Mar. 1992
and engaged in establishment of new mathematical psychology at the Graduate School of Mathematical Sciences, the University of Tokyo from Apr. 1992 to Mar. 2010 and at home from Apr. 2010 on,
and in the meantime
obtained a doctorate in mathematics at the University of Tokyo for the dissertation Characterizations of linear groups of low rank, J. Fac. Sci. Univ. Tokyo 23, 1976,
published the monograph MATHEMATICAL PSYCHOLOGY: Thinking Machines, Logic, Languages and Algebra in Japanese through WWW from Dec.1997 on,
maked its abridged and partly improved English versions, esp. published the monograph MATHEMATICAL NOOLOGY: Intellectual Machines, Logic, Tongues and Algebra through WWW from Spring 2008 on,
published a related paper FOUNDATIONS OF EUSOPHY: A Proscience General Concept of God with a Mathematical Model through WWW from Christmas Day 2014 on,
and published a related note FROM GRAPHS TO ALGEBRAS: From What We See to What We Penetrate.
